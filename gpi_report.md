  - Confidence: High

## P.T.

- Year: 2014
- Platform: PlayStation 4
- GPI Score: 34.75%
- Preservation Status: High Risk
- Rarity Score: 95%
- Rarity Status: Extremely Rare
- Collector Alert: High Priority

### Preservation Evidence

Delisted from the PlayStation Store, difficult to legally access, but heavily documented and culturally significant.

### Rarity Reason

- Playable Access: 5
  - Reason: The game was delisted and is no longer normally available through official storefronts, making legal access extremely limited.
  - Confidence: High
- Platform Dependency: 10
  - Reason: Access depends heavily on specific PlayStation 4 systems or accounts where the game was already installed.
  - Confidence: High
- Documentation: 65
  - Reason: Although access is poor, the game is heavily documented through videos, articles, analysis, fan discussion, and horror game retrospectives.
  - Confidence: High
- Technical Preservation: 20
  - Reason: The game has no public source code or official technical preservation route, and its delisted status creates additional preservation risk.
  - Confidence: Medium
- Community Preservation: 90
  - Reason: The game remains highly discussed, analysed, recreated, and remembered by horror fans and game design communities.
  - Confidence: High
- Cultural Value: 95
  - Reason: The game became one of the most famous horror demos ever released due to its connection to Silent Hills, its design impact, and its unusual delisting history.
  - Confidence: High

  - Confidence: High
